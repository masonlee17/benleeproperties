Ft., 7,993 Sq. Ft. Lot T his 3 bedroom/3 bath treasure of a home in the heart of Beverlywood, is an iconic example of postwar Los Angeles architecture.

It has an added bonus of original features and an abundance of unique personality traits sadly missing from most homes built today.

Built in 1950, most everything in this single story home is original, including the hardwood floors and a variety of intricate, antique light fixtures.

Many of the rooms offer special wall coverings, each in different and interesting textures.

The bedrooms each have nice sized closets and the master bedroom includes a marble vanity and built-in shelves and drawers in one of the closets.

The den, which has a charming wet bar, opens to the grassy backyard and a covered patio.

This lovely house is situated in the award winning Castle Heights School District.

This spacious family home will not be on the market for long!

Ft. I f it’s your dream to live steps from the beach, wake up hearing the ocean and watching the waves crash against the sand, then fantasize no more because this is the home for you.

Spacious and vast with astounding height and light, this 2 bedroom/3 bath townhome in Playa Vista is luxury living at its finest.

Soaringly high ceilings and surrounded by windows, this 2116 square foot home offers a stone fire place, formal living and dining rooms, a remodeled and modern kitchen with top-of-the-line stainless appliances, marble countertops and designer backsplash.

The other bedroom on this level has an attached bathroom, generously sized closet and a private balcony, also with an ocean view.

Additional amenities include an attached two car garage with a large amount of storage, landscaped front patio, all hardwood floors and walking access to adorable neighborhood shops and restaurants.

This home allows for all your California dreams to come true.
