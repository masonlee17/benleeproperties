How real estate selling should be 

Market Analysis

Ben conducts a rigorous comparative market analysis on every property before setting a price. He studies what homes actually sold for — not just listed — factoring in condition, days on market, and buyer psychology. This precision pricing strategy is why Ben's listings sell faster and for more than neighborhood averages.

Property Review & Preparation

Before a single showing, Ben walks your property and gives you a frank assessment of what to fix, what to skip, and what to showcase. Drawing on 15+ years and hundreds of transactions, he knows exactly what today's buyers are looking for — and how to make your home match that picture.

Assigning Realtor

Ben doesn't just close deals fast — he represents your interests with the precision of a trained attorney. As a licensed California broker and former lawyer, Ben negotiates from a position of expertise that most agents simply can't match. His clients consistently net more.

Cleaning & Staging

Real estate property cleaning and staging is the process of preparing a home for sale. The goal of this process is to make the property more attractive to buyers by making it look as if it has been lived in.

This can be achieved through general cleaning, decluttering, changing the furniture layout, putting out flowers or other items to make it look homey.

Preparing for market

Ben evaluates every seller's timeline, equity position, and neighborhood comps before a single photo is taken. Here's how he approaches getting your home ready to attract the strongest possible offers:

  • Deep-dive pricing analysis — what comparable homes actually closed for, not just listed at

  • Targeted buyer outreach — Ben's database of 9,000+ neighborhood households and active buyer network

  • Strategic timing — knowing when to list, when to hold, and when to push for multiple offers
