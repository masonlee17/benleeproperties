Our Team

Ben Lee

Real Estate Broker Associate

Ben Lee gives a competitive advantage to his clients. Ben is an accomplished real estate broker with an exceptional track record and unparalleled knowledge of Los Angeles’ Westside property market. He is also a licensed attorney, which offers his clients an upper hand when it comes time to negotiating deals. A second generation Los Angeles native, Ben is a well known and liked member of the community, with all the connections you need to make the process of buying and selling your home as pleasant and worry free as possible.It’s no wonder that Ben is one of the top producing real estate professionals. Who else is better suited to help you buy or sell your home?

LILLI LEE

Chief Marketing Writer

Lilli is a second generation Los Angeles native and graduated from UCLA with a degree in Art History. Lilli spent over a decade writing and producing for national television shows beginning with Hard Copy, culminating with Extra and freelancing for a variety of reality shows in between. Since marrying Ben Lee in 2001 and becoming a mother to their three young boys beginning in 2004, Lilli has segued her television writing skills to writing all things real estate! Lilli will come to your home and create all the marketing materials necessary for a quick and successful sale. She also oversees the monthly newsletter that gets delivered to over 9000 residences as well as the bi-monthly eBlast.

JOANNA SANCHEZ

CLIENT RELATIONS CO-MANAGER

Joanna has eleven years of real estate management, administration and acquisition experience. She founded her own asset management and real estate investment group in 2008. Before joining the Ben Lee team, she spent three years at Keller Williams & Compass where she managed and audited real estate transactions/contracts for over 300 agents. Part of her duties with Ben Lee include client management/relations as well as office and transaction management. Once a property enters into escrow, Joanna makes sure the clients know what’s behind every door and around every corner, always available to be one step ahead of the process so clients are aware of every part of the journey. Joanna holds a B.A in English-Writing and Legal Studies and is a member of the International English Honor Society, Sigma Tau Delta.

BRITTANY GILBERT

DIGITAL MARKETING STRATEGIST

After graduating with a B.S. degree from Troy University, Brittany spent years learning the art of real estate marketing, eventually earning a position as the Market Director for one of Orlando’s top 3 real estate brokerages. In 2013, she founded an independent digital marketing agency, Velocifox Digital, which gave her the ability to work with Realtors and brokerages across the US, including Ben and his outstanding team. Brittany regularly contributes to industry-specific sites like ActiveRain.com, as well as social media sites and forums including r/Realtor on Reddit. She’s also been featured in The Real Deal NYC magazine, as well as a guest on the Real Estate Safari Radio Podcast.

NICK VITUCCI

Digital Marketing Specialist

Nick is a New York native who has been a part of the Ben Lee team since January 2016. After graduating from Hofstra University with a bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Nick went on to work in the digital marketing space. He worked as a digital marketing assistant through college and has continued to work in digital marketing for over 6 years. Some of Nick’s work includes social media management, website design and PPC management. He manages over $300,000 per month in Google Adwords and Bing Ads spend.
